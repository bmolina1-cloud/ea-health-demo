Why NPI is all we need

Every claim is keyed to the rendering provider's National Provider Identifier and the billing organization's NPI. QGenda tells us which physician covered which shift, EA maps that to their NPI and taxonomy, validates it's active and correctly enrolled with the payer, and stamps it on the 837. No duplicate patient chart, no parallel EHR — just the billing layer the hospital was missing, reading from the systems they already trust.
