What each number means

Net Collection Rate

Collections ÷ (charges − contractual adjustments). The % of legitimately collectible money you actually collected. The #1 RCM health metric.

Days in A/R

Average days a dollar sits unpaid. Lower = faster cash. EA runs at 28 vs industry 35–45.

Clean Claim Rate

% of claims accepted on first submission with no edits. High clean rate = fewer reworks.

First-Pass Resolution

% of claims paid on the first try, no resubmission or appeal needed.

Cost to Collect

Operating cost as % of collections. Industry runs 6–8%; AI automation pulls EA to ~3.8%.

Charge Lag

Days from the visit to the charge dropping. Long lag delays the whole cycle. EA: 1.2 days.
