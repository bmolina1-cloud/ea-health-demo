How the AI keeps you compliant

Auto-benchmark on draft

Every agreement gets FMV-checked at draft time. Compensation above the 75th percentile triggers a justification memo requirement.

Percentile justification memos

When higher comp is warranted (subspecialty, rural, call burden), AI drafts the documentation that defends it.

Annual FMV refresh

Benchmarks update yearly. AI re-checks every active contract and flags any that drift out of range.

Audit-ready trail

Every FMV determination is logged with source, date, percentile, and the analyst (AI or human) who approved it.
